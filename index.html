FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about working with InfyniteTech — from first contact to long-term partnership.

What exactly does InfyniteTech do?+
InfyniteTech is a full-stack technology partner for Indian SMBs and growing enterprises. We combine AI-powered digital transformation, custom software development, digital marketing, managed IT services, BPO operations, and specialized staffing — all under one roof. Think of us as your technology execution partner, not just a vendor.
Where is InfyniteTech located?+
We are headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana — Plot 58/1, Piller No.90, Canara Nagar, Uppal — with a second office in Warangal (102, City Center, Mulugu Road). Our teams work remotely and serve clients nationally across India.
Which industries do you specialize in?+
We have hands-on experience across Healthcare, Banking, Finance, Retail, E-Commerce, Education, E-Learning, Real Estate, Social Networking, and Travel & Tourism. Our solutions are always tailored to the nuances of your industry — not copy-pasted templates.
How do I get started with InfyniteTech?+
Simple — reach out via our Contact form, email us at [email protected], or call +91 9182972327. We'll schedule a free discovery call, understand your goals, and deliver a no-obligation quote within 48 hours.
What is your Digital Transformation service?+
We help businesses cut manual work by up to 60% using AI-powered process automation — without disrupting existing operations. This includes custom portals, dashboards, analytics & BI setup, and board-ready reporting. We migrate legacy systems to modern, cloud-native platforms that scale with your ambitions.
What does your Digital Marketing service include?+
Our digital marketing arm covers SEO & SEM, Social Media Marketing, Content Marketing, Influencer Marketing, and Analytics & ROI tracking. We've helped clients rank on Page 1 of Google with data-driven strategies, and our approach is built for the AI-powered search era.
What does Managed IT Services cover?+
We manage your entire IT infrastructure — including 24/7 Helpdesk, Cybersecurity Monitoring, Cloud Management & Backup, IT Consulting & Roadmapping. Ideal for growing businesses who need enterprise-grade IT without the enterprise price tag.
Do you offer fixed-price projects?+
Yes! We offer fixed-price project engagements, especially for SMBs building web apps, mobile apps, or MVPs. Clear budgets, no hidden surprises, and full accountability. We also offer time-and-material models for more exploratory or evolving projects.
What technologies does InfyniteTech use?+
Our tech stack is battle-tested and broad: React, Next.js, Angular, Vue.js on the frontend; Node.js, Python, Django, FastAPI, Java Spring Boot, Go on the backend; PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, AWS, Azure, GCP for cloud and data. We also integrate AI tools like OpenAI, Claude API, TensorFlow, and LangChain.
How do you handle project delivery and timelines?+
We follow an Agile delivery methodology with transparent sprint planning, regular demos, and clear milestone tracking. You'll always know where your project stands. We prioritize proactive communication over post-deadline surprises.
What types of staffing services do you offer?+
We offer both IT and Non-IT staffing, covering contract, permanent, and healthcare placements. Our fast placement model connects you with top candidates quickly — we've streamlined the entire pipeline from sourcing to background verification to onboarding, with an average 7-day placement time.
What BPO functions can you handle?+
Our BPO services cover Data Entry & Processing, Customer Support, Back Office Operations, Lead Generation, and Background Verifications. We take over non-core functions so your team can focus on strategy, innovation, and growth — with transparent SLAs and regular reporting.
Do you offer internship programs?+
Yes! We run structured Internship Programs for students and fresh graduates across IT, BPO, Digital Marketing, and Staffing domains. Designed to build real-world skills, with mentorship from industry experts, live project work, verified certificates, and PPO opportunities for top performers.
Do you offer 24/7 support?+
Yes — our support team is available 24/7/365. We provide round-the-clock technical support, proactive monitoring, and maintenance services. Your business operations should never face a cloudy day on our watch.
What are your business hours?+
Our office hours are Monday–Friday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM and Saturday: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM. However, for critical IT support, our monitoring and emergency response is active round-the-clock, even on Sundays.
How transparent is InfyniteTech throughout projects?+
Transparency is a core value. We maintain regular progress updates, sprint demos, and open communication channels. You'll have a dedicated point of contact, real-time visibility into deliverables, and clear reporting at every stage — no black boxes.
